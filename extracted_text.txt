[TEXT] WHATFIX SOFTWARE AS A SERVICE AGREEMENT This Software as a Service Agreement (“SaaS Agreement” or “Agreement”) is entered into between Quicko Technosoft Labs Private Limited and its affiliates (“Whatfix”), with its principal place of business at 443, 2nd and 3rd Floor, 14th Main, 17th Cross, Sector-4, HSR Layout, Bangalore-560102, India and Apex Finserv Private Limited, with its principal place of business at 6-3-252/2, Erramanzil, Hyderabad, Telangana 500082, India (“Customer”) on March 15, 2020 (“Effective Date”). 1. Definitions “Platform User” means each Customer employee designated by Customer to serve as user of the Whatfix SaaS platform on Customer’s behalf. “Application” means any software used by the Customer for its internal purposes, including the Customer application identified in the Order Form as Apex Claims Portal. “Order Form” means the order form executed by Whatfix and Customer defining the scope of Customer’s subscription plan, pricing, user counts, and services. “Professional Services” means consulting, implementation, onboarding, training, and configuration services provided by Whatfix as described in the Order Form. “Subscription Term” means the period from March 15, 2020 through March 14, 2021, unless renewed or terminated in accordance with this Agreement. “Subscription Fees” means INR 10,80,000 for the subscription term after discount, as detailed in the Order Form. 2. SaaS Services During the Subscription Term, Customer will receive a non-exclusive, non-transferable right to access and use the Whatfix digital adoption platform solely for its internal business operations and for up to 250 authorized users documented in the Order Form. Whatfix will provide standard support services and onboarding assistance for the creation of guided flows, walkthroughs, and contextual help content for the Customer application. 3. Professional Services Whatfix will provide remote implementation and onboarding services for Apex Claims Portal, including creation support for 35 flows, administrator enablement, and training sessions for Customer stakeholders. The professional service fee payable by Customer for these services is INR 2,50,000. 4. Restrictions Customer shall not copy, reverse engineer, sublicense, rent, lease, resell, or otherwise exploit the SaaS Services except as expressly permitted under this Agreement. Customer shall not use the SaaS Services to build a competing product or provide time-sharing or service bureau services to third parties. 5. Customer Responsibilities Customer will provide timely access to its development and training environments, nominate business and technical contacts, and supply required Customer Content and process information necessary for implementation. Customer is responsible for the acts and omissions of its Platform Users and for ensuring that any Customer Content provided does not infringe third-party rights or violate applicable law. 6. Orders and Payment Customer shall order SaaS Services pursuant to the Order Form executed on March 15, 2020. Unless otherwise stated, Whatfix shall invoice Customer on the Order Form effective date and Customer shall pay undisputed invoices within 30 days of receipt. All fees are exclusive of applicable taxes, and payment shall be made in INR. 7. Term and Termination The Agreement begins on the Effective Date and continues until terminated in accordance with its terms. The Subscription Term will automatically renew for successive terms unless either party gives written notice of non-renewal at least 30 days before expiry of the then-current term. 8. Service Level Agreement The applicable service levels and support commitments are governed by Whatfix Support Terms referenced in this Agreement. 9. Warranties Whatfix warrants that the SaaS Services will be performed in a professional manner substantially in accordance with the documentation, subject to the limitations and disclaimers set out in the Agreement. 10. Limitation of Liability Neither party shall be liable for indirect, incidental, special, or consequential damages, and aggregate liability will not exceed the fees paid or payable by Customer during the 12 months preceding the claim, except as otherwise stated for specified obligations. 11. Indemnification Whatfix will defend Customer against specified third-party IP infringement claims relating to the SaaS Services, and Customer will defend Whatfix against specified claims relating to Customer Content, subject to the conditions in the Agreement.
